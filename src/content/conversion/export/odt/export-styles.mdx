When exporting to ODT, you can define custom styles that will be applied to the exported document. This is useful when you want to have a consistent look and feel across your documents.

<Callout title="Requires Export DOCX extension" variant="warning">
  Style overrides for ODT export require the `@tiptap-pro/extension-export-docx` extension to be installed and configured in your editor. The ODT export uses DOCX generation under the hood before converting to ODT. Without it, the `styleOverrides` and element override options will have no effect.
</Callout>
