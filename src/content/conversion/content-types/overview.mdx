Every document feature behaves differently as it moves through import, editor rendering, and export. These pages document exactly what happens at each stage, what extensions you need, and what to expect.

For a quick overview of what is and is not supported, see the [Supported features](/conversion/getting-started/feature-support-matrix) matrix.
