### Document Operations

- **Reading**: The AI can read and analyze document content in chunks, handling large documents efficiently
- **Editing**: Precisely edit specific sections without replacing entire content
- **Inserting**: Stream content directly into the editor
- **Suggestions**: Review AI-generated changes before accepting or rejecting them

### Advanced Capabilities

- **Schema awareness**: The AI understands custom nodes and marks in your document
- **Selection-aware prompts**: Operations work on selected text or the entire document
- **Change tracking**: Google Docs-style accept/reject workflow for AI edits
