## Capabilities

The AI Toolkit includes the building blocks for any custom AI functionality:

- **Tools** for building [AI agents](/content-ai/capabilities/ai-toolkit/agents) capable of complex tasks.
- [**Workflows**](/content-ai/capabilities/ai-toolkit/workflows) where the AI has a single, well-defined task: insert content, proofread the document, and more.

<Callout title="Compatible with any AI model" variant="info">
  Use the AI Toolkit with any AI model capable of producing text, including open source and
  self-hosted models.
</Callout>
