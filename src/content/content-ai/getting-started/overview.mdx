The Content AI extensions provide an easy integration of AI features like smart autocompletion, proofreading, image generation, and more.

- **AI editor extensions:** Extensions handle the complexity of streaming, diffing, and UI decoration so you can focus on your business logic.
- **Plug-and-play with OpenAI:** Point the extensions to your Content AI App ID and integrate it with your OpenAI account.
- **Custom LLM ready:** Override the resolver hooks to call your custom AI LLM backend.

## Content AI extensions

Our AI extensions work out-of-the-box with Tiptap Cloud, so you can deploy them without an AI backend, but they are also easily extendable to work with your custom backend and LLMs.
