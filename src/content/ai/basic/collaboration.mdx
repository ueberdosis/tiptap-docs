The [Basic AI Generation](/ai/basic/overview) extension uses AI models to generate content. The content will be seen by all connected users once it is inserted into the editor.

However, to preview the AI responses before they are inserted, you can [store them in the extension storage](/ai/basic/text-generation/manage-responses). While in that state, the responses are not seen by other connected users. When the user accepts the response, it will be added the editor content and visible to all connected users.

Likewise, [autocompletion suggestions](/ai/basic/text-generation/autocompletion) are only visible to the user who triggered them, and they are not displayed to other users until the user accepts them.
